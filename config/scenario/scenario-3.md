# Scenario 3: Pop-up Questions

## What to Expect

During your reading session, you will receive **periodic pop-up questions** that interrupt your reading.

### How It Works

- Every 45 seconds (approximately), a pop-up will appear with a question
- Each pop-up has a **multiple-choice question** about the article
- Select your answer and click "Submit Answer"
- You'll receive a **maximum of 3 pop-ups** during the reading session
- After submitting, the pop-up closes and you continue reading

### Example Pop-up

```
Question

Q: According to the article, what is 'attentional fragmentation'?

○ A type of learning disability
○ Habitual difficulty with sustained focus from regular media multitasking
○ A computer software error
○ The ability to focus on multiple things at once

[Submit Answer]
```

## Your Task

- **Read the article naturally** - pop-ups will appear automatically
- **Answer each question** when they appear
- **Try to continue reading** after each interruption
- Notice how the interruptions affect your reading experience

## Important Notes

- The pop-ups are **interruptions** that simulate distractions
- Answer honestly - just select an option and continue
- You cannot skip pop-ups when they appear
- Notice how these interruptions impact your focus
- There is no time limit for reading
