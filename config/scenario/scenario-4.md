# Scenario 4: AI Assistant + Pop-up Interruptions

## What to Expect

During your reading session, you will have access to an **AI reading assistant** AND experience **pop-up interruptions**. This simulates reading with both helpful technology and distractions.

### How It Works

You have access to two features:

#### 1. AI Reading Assistant (3-dot button on the right)
- **Click the button** whenever you want AI help
- Opens a **sidebar** with AI questions and quick actions
- Choose from questions with **three answer options**
- Get **detailed explanations** to help understand concepts
- Also has **quick actions** like "Summarize article" at the bottom

#### 2. Pop-up Interruptions
- Appear every **~45 seconds** automatically
- Present **multiple-choice questions** about the article
- You submit your answer and continue
- Maximum of **3 pop-ups** during the session
- Simulate **distractions** during reading

### Example Interactions

**AI Assistant (sidebar):**
```
Click "•••" button → Sidebar opens
→ Click a question or quick action
→ See 3 options → Select one
→ Get explanation → Continue reading
```

**Pop-up:**
```
Pop-up appears automatically
→ Select an answer → Submit → Continue reading
```

## Your Task

- **Read the article naturally**
- **Use the AI assistant** (•••button) when you want help
- **Answer pop-ups** when they appear
- **Notice how both features** affect your reading experience
- Try to **understand and remember** the content

## Important Notes

- This scenario has **both AI help and interruptions**
- The AI sidebar is **optional** - use it when you want
- Pop-ups **interrupt automatically** - you must answer them
- Try to engage naturally with both
- Notice how having both assistance and interruptions affects your focus
- There is no time limit for reading

## Strategy Suggestions

- Don't rush - take time to read AI explanations
- Try to answer quiz questions accurately
- Keep track of the main ideas as you read
- Notice how the interruptions affect your focus
