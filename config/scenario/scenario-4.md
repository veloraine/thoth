# Experiment Instruction

**Welcome!**
Thank you for participating in this experiment.

This experiment examines how AI-assisted reading affects students' reading comprehension under media multitasking conditions. Your participation is voluntary, and all data collected will remain confidential and used solely for academic research purposes.

---

**Part 1: Main experiment**
- You will read the provided article within **6 minutes** (timer will be shown on the screen).
- Please read the article **as if you are studying material for an exam.**
- You will be **tested on your deep understanding of the content.**
- You will have **access to an AI reading assistant.**
- **The AI reading assistant is designed** <span style="color: #dc3545; font-weight: bold; text-decoration: underline;">to support your understanding, not replace your reading.</span>
- You should **read the article yourself first, and use the AI to enhance your understanding.**
- Please **use the AI reading assistant** <span style="color: #dc3545; font-weight: bold; text-decoration: underline;">at least twice</span> during the reading task, in a way that meaningfully supports your understanding.
- You are <span style="color: #dc3545; font-weight: bold;">required</span> to <span style="color: #dc3545; font-weight: bold;">respond to incoming messages as soon as they appear on the screen, by selecting the response that best fits the conversations, as if you are having a real conversation with your friends or classmates</span> and continue your reading.
- Please <span style="color: #dc3545; font-weight: bold; font-style: italic;">focus</span> and <span style="color: #dc3545; font-weight: bold;">complete</span> the reading task carefully.

**Part 2: Post reading task survey**
After the reading task, you will complete:
- Short metacomprehension questions (self evaluation of your understanding about the article).
- A perceived cognitive load questionnaires.

**Part 3: Reading comprehension test**
- You will complete a reading comprehension test within **5 minutes**.

**Part 4: Post experiment questionnaire**
- You will complete a short post experiment questions and demographic.
