# Welcome to the Reading Study

Thank you for participating in this research study!

## What to Expect

This study consists of several parts:

### 1. Mini Reading Test (2 minutes)
You will first complete a brief timed reading comprehension test. This will assess your baseline reading ability.

### 2. Reading Session
You will read an academic article titled "AI Assisted Reading Under Media Multitasking Condition: Impact On University Student Reading Comprehension."

**Important:** Depending on your randomly assigned condition, you may experience different types of assistance or interruptions during reading. Please engage with these naturally as you would in a real study situation.

### 3. Post-Reading Questionnaires
After reading, you will answer several questionnaires about:
- Your reading experience
- Comprehension of the article
- Your background and habits

## Instructions

- Please complete this study in one sitting in a quiet environment
- Use a desktop or laptop computer (not a mobile device) if possible
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- Do not use external resources or search for information during the study
- Answer all questions honestly - there are no right or wrong answers for subjective questions

## Time Commitment

The entire study should take approximately **20-25 minutes** to complete.
