# Participant Consent Form

## Purpose
You are invited to voluntarily participate in this experiment. The purpose of this study is to examine university students' reading comprehension during on-screen reading assisted by an AI assistant under a media multitasking condition.

## Eligibility
You must currently be a university student enrolled in a bachelor's, master's, or PhD program.

## Compensation
You will receive the payment for completing the experiment. If you do not complete the experiment, you will not receive any payment.

## Risks and Benefits
This experiment has no known health risks or direct benefits. The activities are similar to normal academic tasks, such as reading an academic paper.

## Confidentiality
Your personal information will be kept confidential. Only the researcher involved in this experiment will have access to your data. Your name will not appear in any research reports.
